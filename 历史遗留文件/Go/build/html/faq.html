处理中文乱码¶

参见

https://studygolang.com/articles/8897

导入github.com/axgle/mahonia库

enc := mahonia.NewDecoder("gbk")
goStr:= enc.ConvertString(robotgo.GetTitle())
fmt.Println(goStr)

注解

robot.GetTitle()返回C.GoString的值，遇到中文会显示乱码
